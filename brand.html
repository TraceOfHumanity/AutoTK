Acura vehicles

Founded in 1986, Acura is marketed as a luxury car brand of the American Honda Car Company. Acura competes with the best German and US car makers in the premium segment and produces cars with high-end comfort features followed by powerful V6 engines and automatic transmission. Best known for their eccentric design, Acura vehicles are equipped with advanced suspension systems and latest driving electronic aids providing the cars with top quality driving characteristics. Cars produced by Acura are exclusively available on the US and Canadian car markets making the brand highly valued in the whole world.
